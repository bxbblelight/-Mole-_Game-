How to Play

  • Click the Start Game button to begin.
  • Moles pop up from holes in different colors:
    • Gold mole: +3 points
    • Brown mole: +1 point
    • Red mole: -1 point
  • Click on a mole while it's visible to score or lose points.
  • Your goal is to get the highest score possible!
